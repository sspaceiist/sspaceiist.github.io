Pilot G2 Grace Payload

The Pilot G2 Grace Payload is an advanced writing instrument developed by the Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) under the auspices of ISRO. Designed for precision and performance, this pen was crafted to showcase the synergy between space technology and everyday objects.

What makes the Pilot G2 Grace Payload unique is its connection to India's space program. It was developed as part of a collaboration with ISRO, demonstrating how innovative technologies can be applied beyond their original scope, enriching daily life with cutting-edge advancements.
